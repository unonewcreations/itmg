Running Terms

10-K pace
10-K pace, when used in a workout to describe how fast to run is simply the pace of a runner's last 10-K race.
5-K/8-K/10-K
K is for kilometers, 1000 meters. A 5-K is equal to 3.1 miles; 8-K is 4.96 miles; 10-K os equal to 6.2 miles.
400 meters
Equivalent to a quater mile or 1 lap around a standard track.
800 meters
Equivalent to a half-mile or 2 laps around a standard track.
